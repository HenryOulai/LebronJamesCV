LeBron James

My name is LeBron James, also known as the GOAT (Greatest of All Time), I'm a professional basketball player currently playing for the Los Angeles Lakers in the NBA. I'm from Akron, Ohio but now reside in Los Angeles with my family. Off the court, I'm very involved in the community and am a positive role model for young people through my own foundation, which focuses on supporting education and improving the lives of those in need.
